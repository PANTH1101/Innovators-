BHMS Information

About BHMS

BHMS (Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery) is a 5.5-year undergraduate degree focused on homeopathic medicine, including a 1-year mandatory internship. Graduates can work as homeopathic doctors, researchers, or in wellness centers.

Career Opportunities

BHMS graduates can work as homeopathic doctors, researchers, medical officers, or wellness consultants. They can also pursue higher studies for specialization in homeopathy.
