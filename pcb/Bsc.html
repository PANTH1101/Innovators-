BSc Biology Information

About BSc Biology

BSc Biology is a 3-year undergraduate degree that covers various aspects of biological sciences, including genetics, microbiology, botany, and zoology. It prepares students for careers in healthcare, research, and environmental sciences.

Career Opportunities

BSc Biology graduates can work in healthcare, research, environmental science, and biotechnology. Job roles include Microbiologist, Biotechnologist, Environmental Scientist, Lab Technician, and Research Analyst.
