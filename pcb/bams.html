BAMS Information

About BAMS

BAMS (Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine and Surgery) is a 5.5-year undergraduate degree focusing on Ayurvedic medicine, including a 1-year mandatory internship. Graduates can practice as Ayurvedic doctors or work in research, hospitals, and wellness centers.

Career Opportunities

BAMS graduates can work as Ayurvedic doctors, researchers, hospital consultants, or in wellness clinics. They can also pursue higher studies in specialized fields of Ayurveda.
