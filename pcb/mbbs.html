MBBS Information

About MBBS

MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) is a 5.5-year professional degree in medical science, including a 1-year mandatory internship. Graduates become doctors and can work in hospitals, clinics, research, and public health sectors.

Top Medical Colleges in Gujarat

    A premier government medical college known for its excellent academics and clinical exposure.

  • 2. AIIMS, Rajkot

    One of the newest AIIMS institutions offering top-notch medical education.

  • 3. Medical College Baroda

  • 4. M.P. Shah Medical College, Jamnagar

    Offers comprehensive medical training with strong clinical exposure.

  • 5. Sumandeep Vidyapeeth, Vadodara

  • 6. KIRC Medical College, Kalol

    Emerging as a strong medical college with modern infrastructure.

  • 7. Dharmsinh Desai University (DDU), Nadiad

    Known for its quality education and medical training programs.

  • 8. GMERS Medical College, Patan

    Part of GMERS, providing excellent medical education and hospital training.

  • 9. AMC MET Medical College, Ahmedabad

    Offers high-quality education and medical research opportunities.

  • 10. Pramukhswami Medical College (PSMC), Karamsad

    Well known for its academic excellence and research in medical sciences.

Placements

MBBS graduates can work in top hospitals like AIIMS, Apollo Hospitals, Fortis, Civil Hospitals, and government health sectors. Many also pursue post-graduate specializations for higher career opportunities.
