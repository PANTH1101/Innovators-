BBA Information

About BBA

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) is a 3-year undergraduate program focusing on business management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship. It prepares students for careers in corporate management, startups, and leadership roles.

Career Opportunities

BBA graduates can work in corporate firms, startups, banking, and marketing sectors. Job roles include Business Analyst, Marketing Manager, Financial Consultant, HR Manager, and Entrepreneur.
