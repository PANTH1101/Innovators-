B.Com Information

About B.Com

B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) is a 3-year undergraduate program that provides students with knowledge in finance, accounting, taxation, business law, and management. It prepares students for careers in banking, finance, auditing, and business sectors.

Career Opportunities

B.Com graduates can work in banking, finance, accounting, taxation, and auditing sectors. Job roles include Accountant, Financial Analyst, Tax Consultant, Auditor, and Business Manager.
