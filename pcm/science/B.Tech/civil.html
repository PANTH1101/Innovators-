Civil Engineering Information

About Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as buildings, roads, bridges, and water systems. It plays a crucial role in urban development and sustainability.

Course Duration

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Civil Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate program.

Placements

Top Civil Engineering colleges in Gujarat have strong placement records with companies like L&T, Tata Projects, Adani, Shapoorji Pallonji, and Reliance Infrastructure hiring graduates. Placement opportunities depend on student skills and college reputation.
