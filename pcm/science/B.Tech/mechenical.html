Mechanical Engineering Information

About Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical Engineering is one of the broadest engineering fields, covering the design, development, and manufacturing of mechanical systems. It plays a vital role in industries such as automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, and robotics.

Course Duration

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Mechanical Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate program.

Placements
