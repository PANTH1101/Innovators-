Information Technology Information

About Information Technology

Information Technology (IT) focuses on software development, networking, database management, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. IT professionals work in various industries, including software engineering, data science, and IT infrastructure.

Course Duration

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Information Technology is a 4-year undergraduate program.

Placements
