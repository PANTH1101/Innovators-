Electronics & Communication Engineering Information

About Electronics & Communication Engineering

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) involves designing, developing, and testing electronic circuits, communication systems, and signal processing technologies. Graduates can work in telecommunications, embedded systems, IoT, and semiconductor industries.

Course Duration

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Electronics & Communication Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate program.

  • 8. Vishwakarma Government Engineering College (VGEC), Ahmedabad

  • 9. Marwadi University, Rajkot

    SVIT offers specialized ECE programs with experienced faculty and hands-on learning opportunities.

Placements

Top ECE colleges in Gujarat have excellent placement records with companies like Texas Instruments, Qualcomm, Intel, TCS, Infosys, and Wipro hiring graduates. Placement rates depend on student performance and college reputation.
