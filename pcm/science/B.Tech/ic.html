Instrumentation & Control Engineering Information

About Instrumentation & Control Engineering

Instrumentation & Control Engineering focuses on the design, development, and maintenance of instruments and control systems used in industries. Graduates can work in automation, robotics, process control, and industrial instrumentation.

Course Duration

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Instrumentation & Control Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate program.
