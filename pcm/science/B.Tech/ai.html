AI & ML Engineering Information

About AI & ML Engineering

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML) Engineering focuses on developing intelligent systems, algorithms, and applications for automation, data analysis, and problem-solving in various industries.

Course Duration

Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) or Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in AI & ML Engineering is a 4-year undergraduate program.
