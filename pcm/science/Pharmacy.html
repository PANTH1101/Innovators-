B.Pharm Information

About B.Pharm

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) is a 4-year undergraduate degree focusing on pharmaceutical sciences, drug development, and healthcare. Graduates work in the pharmaceutical industry, research, hospitals, and regulatory agencies.
