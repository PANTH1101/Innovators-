BFA Information

About BFA

BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts) is a 4-year undergraduate degree focusing on visual and performing arts, including painting, sculpture, graphic design, and applied arts.

Career Opportunities

BFA graduates can work as Artists, Graphic Designers, Illustrators, Art Directors, and Multimedia Specialists. They can find employment in advertising agencies, museums, publishing houses, and freelance projects.
