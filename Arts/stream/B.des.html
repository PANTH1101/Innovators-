B.Des Information

About B.Des

B.Des (Bachelor of Design) is a 4-year undergraduate degree that focuses on design principles, creativity, and innovation. It prepares students for careers in fashion, product design, UI/UX, and more.

Career Opportunities

B.Des graduates can work in fashion design, interior design, industrial design, UI/UX, animation, and branding. Job roles include Graphic Designer, Product Designer, UX Designer, Art Director, and Creative Consultant.
