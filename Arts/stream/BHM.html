BHM Information

About BHM

BHM (Bachelor of Hotel Management) is a 3 to 4-year undergraduate degree focused on hospitality, hotel administration, catering, and tourism, preparing students for careers in the hospitality industry.

Top BHM Colleges in Gujarat

  • 1. C.Z. Patel College of Business and Management

    Offers specialized courses in hospitality and hotel management.

  • 2. Panache Academy

    Known for its professional training in hotel and tourism management.

  • 3. Bhagwan Mahavir University (BMU), Surat

    Provides comprehensive programs in hospitality and tourism studies.

  • 4. CVM University

    Focuses on practical learning and hospitality business management.

  • 5. Bhagwan Mahavir College of Hotel Management

    Equipped with modern facilities and industry-focused training.

Career Opportunities

BHM graduates can work as Hotel Managers, Event Managers, Chefs, Travel Consultants, and Resort Managers. They find employment in luxury hotels, cruise ships, restaurants, and tourism agencies.
