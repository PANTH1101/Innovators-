BA Information

About BA

BA (Bachelor of Arts) is a 3-year undergraduate degree focusing on humanities, social sciences, and liberal arts. It provides diverse career opportunities in education, media, public administration, and research.

Career Opportunities

BA graduates can work in education, journalism, public relations, civil services, and research. Job roles include Content Writer, Social Worker, Teacher, Civil Service Officer, and Journalist.
